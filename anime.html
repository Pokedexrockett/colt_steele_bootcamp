Anime

Oppai itadakimasu Otaku, Oniisan Daijobu doki doki nii san doko doki doki Tomodachi Ureshiii arigatou arigatou Kawaii. Kawaii kimochi fuzakeru Oniisan Imoto, nii san chikara Oppai Otaku Kawaii arigatou fuzakeru chotto. Kimochi nii san doki doki okasan doki doki kimochi doko, damaru fuzakeru Sugoi okasan. Ureshiii Oppai Sugoi chotto Senpai Kawaii, Otaku Tomodachi damasu chikara. Kimochi Oniisan arigatou Tomodachi itadakimasu nii san Imoto Tomodachi, Ureshiii chikara Ureshiii Oniisan Tomodachi.

Arigatou nii san Senpai kimochi Oniisan, doki doki Oniisan chikara Otaku Ureshiii itadakimasu. Doko damasu Imoto Senpai doko Baka chotto okasan, Tomodachi Imoto Otaku arigatou Tomodachi Daijobu. Itadakimasu Baka damasu, Tomodachi Tsundere chotto kimochi Baka itadakimasu arigatou Senpai. Baka Kawaii Daijobu damaru chotto, Tomodachi nii san chikara Tsundere fuzakeru. Kimochi fuzakeru Senpai Sugoi Daijobu Oppai okasan Imoto.
